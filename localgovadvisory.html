Local Government Advisory

Anchor Project Solutions and its associates have been providing strategic and technical advisory services to local governments and agencies in Alberta, Canada, and Southern Africa since 2020. Leveraging our team's extensive public sector expertise, we enable clients to effectively plan and achieve their strategic objectives. Our commitment to delivering expert advice and adding value has fostered strong relationships, driving repeat engagements and long-term partnerships.

With profound knowledge of private and public infrastructure, climate resilience & adaptation, land development strategies, skills development, and empowering women and youth, APS and its associates are at the forefront of driving impact-driven results. We offer flexible support across various project stages, including:

  • Concept review and feasibility assessment
  • Baseline financial forecasting
  • Project charter development
  • Representation of client interests to ensure successful outcome realization

Our expertise and experience position us to provide targeted support where needed, ensuring clients achieve their goals and realize forecasted outcomes.

Core Focus Areas

Climate Change & Adaptation Resilience

Our local government advisory team can assist with reviewing the impact and risk on land and assets under a range of climate change prediction models. We identify property and land-based solutions that support and achieve resilient communities.

Infrastructure Assets

Our team can assist from project inception — including preliminary evaluations, budgeting, site selection, zoning, and expedited approval processes — through to implementation, land servicing, and full project management.

Infrastructure Asset Development

Project Inception:

  • Preliminary evaluations and advisory services
  • Budgeting and forecasting
  • Strategic planning (site selection and zoning/rezoning)
  • Facilitating approval processes with regulatory agencies

Implementation:
