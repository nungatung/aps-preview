Who We Are

Meet the team behind Anchor Project Solutions — a collective of passionate professionals dedicated to delivering infrastructure, housing, and advisory excellence across Canada and Africa. With diverse expertise and a shared commitment to impact, we’re building stronger, more resilient communities through every project we lead.

Winston Rossouw

Founder, CEO & President: Anchor Project Solutions LTD

A seasoned professional engineer (P.Eng, MSc., PMP) with over 20 years' experience. Winston is renowned to foster solid partnerships with small to medium-sized businesses and organizations to drive innovation and growth through data-driven strategies. With a unique blend of engineering expertise, practical problem-solving skills, and a deep respect for the natural and built environments, he guides, aids and define the critical intersection of climate change and infrastructure. His strategic leadership guides businesses in developing sustainably conscious projects, prioritizing reduced environmental impact, ethical practices, and a robust circular economy. By engaging diverse stakeholder groups, he delivers results that drive local solutions with global impact. He is passionate about advancing sustainable practices, staying current on industry trends, and promoting a culture of recycling, reuse and repurposing.

Jerome Brophy

Principal Director and Project Manager (B.Hons Public Administration): Anchor Project Solutions-Africa PTY LTD.

Jerome offers nearly three decades of experience in engineering and development facilitation within municipal environments, Jerome is a respected professional known for his innovative approach and problem-solving expertise. His Key Strengths include:

  • Extensive Experience: Local government policies, procedures, procurement practices, and planning bylaws
  • Analytical Mindset: Precision-driven approach to tackling complex engineering challenges
  • Strategic Planning: Expertise in optimizing resources to drive meaningful change
  • Sustainable Development: Advocacy for environmentally conscious engineering practices

His Impact and work focus on community upliftment through engineering and development facilitation, creating lasting results. He mentors future engineers and community leaders, fostering innovation and practical problem-solving skills. Throughout Jerome's career he received multiple awards for his contributions to engineering, development facilitation, improving the lives of youth and women and providing ongoing mentorship.
