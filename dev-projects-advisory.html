Development Projects Advisory

Anchor Project Solutions offers specialist advisory services tailored to client-focused development projects. Our role is not as a party to the development itself, but rather as an independent advisor and client liaison throughout key project stages—from feasibility and preparation to implementation and close-out. We provide strategic guidance, ensuring informed decision-making and seamless project execution.

APS has a strong track record in property development advisory services, supporting a diverse range of land development projects—from brownfield and greenfield developments to urban renewal and regeneration initiatives. Our expertise spans both public and private sector engagements, ensuring comprehensive support at every stage from project initiation to completion.

Property Due Diligence & Development Feasibility Studies

APS delivers comprehensive due diligence reports to assess development sites with precision and expertise. Through collaboration with our network of associated professionals, we conduct feasibility studies to evaluate viable development options, ensuring strategic alignment with site-specific conditions, regulatory frameworks, and market trends.

Project Management

From inception to completion, APS can orchestrate the full design and delivery lifecycle, guiding projects with meticulous oversight. Our services encompass procurement management, including the selection of design consultants, general contractors, and specialized professionals—ensuring seamless execution at every stage. As your development partner, we foster accountability, efficiency, and innovation to bring your vision to life.

Development Risk Mitigation Analysis

In today's dynamic and uncertain climate, APS helps clients implement Development Management Risk Mitigation strategies designed to safeguard projects while sustaining momentum.
