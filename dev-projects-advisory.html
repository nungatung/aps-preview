Development Projects Advisory

Anchor Project Solution's advisory services includes specialist client-focussed development projects advisory services, where we support clients, not as a party to the development, but rather in an independent advisory capacity and client liaison during project feasibility or preparation, implementation and close out stages.

APS has a proven track record in property development advisory services across a variety of land development projects—ranging from brownfield to greenfield, including urban renewal and regeneration projects for both public and private sector clients. Our advisory includes feasibility evaluations, business case development, master planning, project facilitation, and ongoing delivery oversight to ensure objectives are met at every stage.

Property Due Diligence & Development Feasibility Studies

APS provides comprehensive due diligence reports on development sites. Together with our associated professionals, we conduct feasibility studies to evaluate viable development options based on site-specific conditions.

Project Management

From project inception to completion, APS manages the full design and delivery lifecycle. This includes procurement of design consultants, main contractors, and other specialist consultants. We act as your development partner every step of the way.

Development Risk Mitigation Analysis

APS works with clients to put Development Management Risk Mitigation strategies in place. Especially in today’s uncertain climate, we review potential risks and their implications, then provide clear recommendations to help navigate the least-risk pathways forward. Our aim is to safeguard projects while keeping momentum strong.
