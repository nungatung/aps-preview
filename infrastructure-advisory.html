Planning and Infrastructure Technical Advisory

At APS, we recognize that current funding levels are inadequate to meet infrastructure needs which could lead to deteriorating infrastructure mainly because of a lack of adequate intime investment. Without a strategic approach we recognize that competing factors such as social, environmental, and economic factors become increasingly difficult to balance. It is for these reasons that a framework is essential to articulate the value of infrastructure planning and maintenance. APS can help develop a framework which will focus on prioritizing Infrastructure investments, optimizing funding allocation and ensuring sustainable infrastructure planning and management.

Grant Funding Expertise

We specialize in securing grant funding for infrastructure projects, aligning with clients' strategic plans. Our services include:

  • Grant Research: Identifying relevant funding opportunities
  • Project Data Support: Providing data and strategies to strengthen grant applications
  • Grant Writing and Submission: Assembling and submitting comprehensive grant proposals

Our grant funding expertise to date has helped our clients to:

  • Access Funding: Securing financial support for infrastructure projects
  • Achieve Strategic Objectives: Aligning grant funding with maintenance and capital plans
  • Maximize Success: Increasing chances of grant approval through well-crafted proposals

APS provides expert guidance on public and environmental infrastructure supporting commercial, industrial, and housing developments. Our technical support teams assist with infrastructure planning, expansion planning for existing and future networks not limited to:

  • water reticulation

Stakeholder Engagement and Consultation

At APS, we recognize the importance of stakeholder engagement in infrastructure projects. Our approach involves:

  • Early Engagement: Involving stakeholders from project inception
  • Active Listening: Capturing ideas, concerns, and needs
  • Collaborative Solution-Finding: Identifying best-fit solutions
  • Regular Feedback: Keeping stakeholders informed at key project milestones

Our stakeholder engagement approach ensures it embodies inclusive decision-making, arriving at effective solutions and thereby increasing project buy-in.
