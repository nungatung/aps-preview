Planning and Infrastructure Technical Advisory

At APS, we recognize that current funding levels are insufficient to address growing infrastructure demands, leading to potential deterioration due to delayed or inadequate investment. Without a strategic approach, balancing competing priorities—social, environmental, and economic—becomes increasingly challenging. That’s why a structured framework is essential to articulate the value of proactive infrastructure planning and maintenance. APS can help develop a comprehensive framework that prioritizes infrastructure investments, optimizes funding allocation, and ensures sustainable planning and management by aligning investment decisions with long-term resilience and efficiency.

Our Core Services:

We specialize in three key areas of urban planning and governance:

Urban Planning Policies

We develop comprehensive, forward-thinking policies that promote sustainable urban development. Our approach integrates environmental resilience, economic viability, and social inclusivity to ensure cities thrive.

Urban Management Systems

Infrastructure and Technical Advisory

Enabling Infrastructure Solutions

APS provides strategic guidance and technical expertise in infrastructure development, helping clients navigate complex processes to achieve sustainable, resilient outcomes. Our advisory services cover the development of Expressions of Interest (EOIs) and Requests for Proposals (RFPs), grant funding expertise, infrastructure planning, and stakeholder engagement.

EOIs & RFPs Expertise

We assist clients in developing and assembling:

  • Requests for Expressions of Interest (EOIs) to identify potential partners and solutions.
  • Requests for Proposals (RFPs) to attract competitive, high-quality submissions.

Our expertise ensures:

  • Clear Objectives: Well-articulated client goals and project outcomes.
  • Targeted Responses: EOIs and RFPs tailored to specific client needs.
  • Value-Added Submissions: Proposals aligned with client objectives to drive successful outcomes.

Grant Funding Expertise

We specialize in securing grant funding for infrastructure projects, ensuring alignment with clients’ strategic plans. Our comprehensive services include:

  • Grant Research: Identifying relevant funding opportunities tailored to project needs.
  • Project Data Support: Strengthening applications with strategic insights and robust data.
  • Grant Writing & Submission: Crafting well-structured proposals to maximize approval success.

Technical Advisory: Infrastructure Planning & Optimization

APS delivers expert guidance on public and environmental infrastructure supporting commercial, industrial, and housing developments. Our technical support spans planning and expansion for existing and future networks, including:

  • Water Reticulation
  • Sanitary Sewer Networks
  • Stormwater Management
  • Road & Sidewalk Infrastructure

Additionally, we prioritize maintenance strategies, optimizing existing infrastructure to:

  • Enhance life cycle performance.
  • Implement solutions that extend the life cycle of linear infrastructure.

Our enabling infrastructure solutions strengthen:

  • Sustainable Development
  • Efficient Infrastructure Management
  • Community Growth & Accessibility

Stakeholder Engagement & Consultation

Recognizing the vital role of stakeholder engagement in infrastructure projects, APS fosters collaboration through:

  • Early Engagement: Involving stakeholders from project inception to ensure transparency.
  • Active Listening: Capturing ideas, concerns, and needs.
  • Collaborative Solution-Finding: Identifying the best-fit strategies through participatory dialogue.
  • Regular Feedback: Providing continuous updates at key project milestones for enhanced project buy-in.

Our engagement approach empowers communities, aligns objectives, and drives lasting impact in infrastructure development.
