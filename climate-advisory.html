Climate Advisory

Our joint mission is to minimize the human and environmental impacts of climate change by fostering resilience, promoting sustainable practices, and supporting community-led initiatives.

Building Climate Resilience in Communities We specialize in strengthening the resilience of small, medium, and rural communities to the current and near-future impacts of climate change. Our approach involves:

Thematic Programming

  • Engaging communities through positive and inclusive programming
  • Amplifying knowledge through accessible learning programs and resources

Key Initiatives

Practical Short Courses* – addressing knowledge gaps on topics such as:

  • Wildlife and climate change
  • Resilience building
  • Climate services and tools
  • Climate change and tourism

Knowledge Sharing – delivered through podcasts, story maps, policy briefs, and guidance documents based on real-world experiences and lessons learned.

Whole-Community Approach

Our comprehensive climate and disaster risk management approach is grounded in evidence-based knowledge, combining local, cultural, scientific, and technical expertise.

Objectives & Outcomes

  • Foster community-led planning and action
  • Enhance climate resilience and adaptation
  • Reduce disaster risk
