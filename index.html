Impact Driven Innovation, Demonstrated Results.

Anchor Project Solutions is a proudly independent advisory and planning consultancy, connecting communities across Western Canada and Africa. We bring people together to shape resilient infrastructure and deliver sustainable development outcomes.

Meet the Team

Multiple Projects Delivered

Across Western Canada and Africa, Anchor Project Solutions is at the forefront of housing, infrastructure, and community planning—delivering sustainably driven projects that minimize environmental impact and foster economic resilience.

Decades of Experience

With over 30 years collective experience in infrastructure, housing, and development, our advisors bring a powerful blend of strategic insight and technical excellence to every project. Their deep industry knowledge enables us to craft innovative, sustainable solutions that adapt to the changing needs of communities across Western Canada and Africa.

Community-Centric

We are deeply committed to fostering a society where women and youth have the tools, resources, and opportunities to succeed.
Through dedicated initiatives, we promote:

  • Access to Equality – Ensuring fair opportunities and dismantling barriers to success.
  • Economic Empowerment and Opportunities – Providing pathways for financial independence and career growth.
  • Leadership Development and Mentorship – Cultivating strong leaders and creating networks of support.
  • Inclusive and Supportive Environments – Building safe spaces where everyone feels valued and heard.

By focusing on these pillars, we drive meaningful impact that leads to lasting, sustainable change—benefiting individuals,
strengthening communities, and shaping a brighter future that is focused on a whole community approach.
