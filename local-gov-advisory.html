Local Government Advisory

Anchor Project Solutions and its associates have been providing strategic and technical advisory services to local governments and agencies in Alberta, Canada, and Southern Africa since 2020. Leveraging our team's extensive public sector expertise, we enable clients to effectively plan and achieve their strategic objectives. Our commitment to delivering expert advice and adding value has fostered strong relationships, driving repeat engagements and long-term partnerships.

With profound expertise in private and public infrastructure, climate resilience and adaptation, land development strategies, and skills development—particularly in empowering women and youth—APS and its associates lead the way in delivering impact-driven results.

We provide flexible and strategic support across various project stages, ensuring optimal outcomes.
Our services include:

  • Concept review and feasibility assessment to validate project viability
  • Baseline financial forecasting for informed decision-making
  • Project charter development to define clear objectives and execution plans
  • Client representation to safeguard interests and drive successful realization

Leveraging our experience and strategic approach, we offer targeted support precisely where needed, enabling clients to achieve their goals and realize forecasted outcomes with confidence.

Core Focus Areas:

Climate Change & Adaptation Resilience

Our local government advisory team specializes in assessing the impact and risk of climate change on land and assets using various predictive models. We identify property and land-based solutions that foster resilient communities, ensuring sustainability and long-term adaptability.

Infrastructure Assets

From project inception to full implementation, we provide comprehensive advisory services, including preliminary evaluations, budgeting, site selection, zoning, and expedited approval processes. Our expertise ensures efficient land servicing, streamlined development, and successful project management.

Infrastructure Asset Development

Project Inception:

  • Preliminary evaluations and advisory services
  • Budgeting and forecasting
  • Strategic planning (site selection and zoning/rezoning)
  • Facilitating approval processes with regulatory agencies

Implementation:

  • Land servicing and development
  • Full-scale project management

We specialize in accelerating project timelines, navigating complex regulatory landscapes, and mitigating risks. By working closely with clients, we ensure smooth approvals, effective risk management, and successful project outcomes.

Stakeholder Engagement and Consultation

We recognize that successful project delivery requires effective collaboration with diverse stakeholders. Our team develops tailored engagement strategies to ensure project alignment with community and organizational needs.

Key Services:

  • Stakeholder analysis and mapping
  • Customized engagement plans

  • Development of communication materials and strategies

By prioritizing stakeholder engagement, we help clients build trust, manage expectations, and drive project success.
